Bound II Happen

Metalcore band based in Cherkasy, Ukraine
The band was founded in 2024 by two guitarists, Svyatoslav and Oleksiy,
later, that same year, drummer Dmytro and vocalist Alena joined the band.
The band is developing in the direction of covers, and in the future plans to record their first album.
